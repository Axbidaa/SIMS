About

The Student Internship Management System (SIMS) is an employment website specialized in providing students with the right opportunities to gain experience crucial for completing their courses successfully. In a landscape where other employment websites are saturated with a myriad of job listings, SIMS stands out as the ideal platform for students to acquire hands-on experience in their respective industries.

Services

A medium for students to find relevant job openings

A medium for employers to find suitable interns

A medium for students to connect with each other
